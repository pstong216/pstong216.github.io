About

Hi, I am an Undergrad student at Zhejiang University. Previously, I was a visiting research student at Texas A&M University, Dream Lab supervised by Prof. Meng Xia. Before that, I was also a research student at Zhejiang University IDG(Interactive Data Group), supervised by Prof. Yingcai Wu and Prof. Dazhen Deng.

My research interests lie in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), with a strong focus on Human-AI Interaction and Data Visualization. I am particularly interested in exploring the roles that AI technologies and tools play in various human activities, especially in areas like cognition, collaboration, and learning. Through my research, I aim to understand how these technologies influence and enhance human experiences, shaping the ways we think, connect, and learn.
